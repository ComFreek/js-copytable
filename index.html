Welcome to js-copytable

What?

Ever wanted to directly copy a table on a website to a Microsoft Excel Worksheet? Just use js-copytable!

How?

Embed these two files in your HTML document: <script src="ZeroClipboard.min.js"></script> <script src="copytable.js"></script>
